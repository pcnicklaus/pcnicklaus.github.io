I think the main reason I fell ridiculously head-over-heels in love with coding is because it is the truest expression and closest I will ever be to magic. When I code, and it works :), it feels like utter and total magic. Or creation, but then people might say I’m comparing myself to a deity, which I am not doing!


But seriously, I get to take a bunch of words and symbols and after hours of debugging and console.logs, a new world appears on a screen. And this world has depth and complexity.
