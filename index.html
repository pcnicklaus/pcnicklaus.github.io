Address Me

One of the challenges our homeless population faces is the inability to receive mail due to their lack of a mailing address, which is a major problem because most social services require one. Basically, it's caught in a loop. Address Me is a platform where people with a mailing address can offer to share their's. and those in need can place a request for help in a convenient place for them.
